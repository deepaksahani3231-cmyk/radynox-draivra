Good founders do not need more random links.

They need the right resources at the right stage.

## Recommended stack

- Learning communities for founder feedback.
- Operator-focused playbooks for process and hiring.
- Financial planning templates for early runway decisions.
- Lightweight CRM and project tracking tools.

## Selection rule

Choose tools that reduce decision fatigue, not tools that add configuration work.

Your stack should help you decide faster, communicate clearly, and keep momentum.
