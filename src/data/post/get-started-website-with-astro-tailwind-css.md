Launching a website should not take months of debate.

For most early teams, the best site is clear, credible, and easy to navigate.

## Launch checklist

1. Clear one-line positioning.
2. Focused service pages with outcomes.
3. Trust signals: testimonials, founder context, real examples.
4. Fast contact route with visible response expectation.
5. Analytics + basic SEO configured.

## Keep it simple

Your site is a conversation starter, not a full pitch deck.

Ship the clearest version first, then improve based on real questions from users and customers.
